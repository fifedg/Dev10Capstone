Exploratory Questions:


General:
* How many firms are there for different types of insurance?
* Which types of insurance accrue the most cost/profit?
* What are the prices of insurance premiums over the years by state?
* Which are the most profitable firms?

Healthcare:
* Compare statistics between employer provided insurance, vs direct pay?
* Compare between private vs. public health insurance.
* What conditions are common to people who are well insured vs. under or uninsured, if any?
* What factors affect insurance premiums (Machine learning would be good here!)
* How does having a disability affect health insurance premiums?
* Are the healthiest states/areas also the best insured?

Housing:
* Percent of home occupied by renters vs. owners over the years/by state
* Which caused the greatest homeowner’s insurance loss? (ie theft, fire, etc)
* Any prominent spikes that occurred after a significant event? (ie Hurricane Katrina)
* Which areas of housing seem to be growing and which are declining (area, rent vs buy, type of home etc.)
